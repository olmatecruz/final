Max Payne 3

Without a doubt Rockstars best game. Max Payne 3 features a deep and rich narrative, with some of the best gameplay and shooting mechanics to come out in this decade. Though it does not provide a similar tone to the first two games, Max Payne 3 manages to stound out from its predecessors and dropped the dark gritty tone for a more vibrant colour scheme. Though its presentation is bright, the story behind it is one of the darkest peices of fiction. Easily a 5/5.
